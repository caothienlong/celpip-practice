following the War of 1812 against the Americans, the British loyalists recognized the
vulnerability of the St. Lawrence to American attack. In 1826, Lieutenant Colonel John

could serve as a secure military supply route between Montreal and Kingston. The project

the waterway, while the Royal Engineers oversaw its design. The skilled work was done
